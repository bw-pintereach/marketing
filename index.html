As a researcher, it's difficult to keep track of articles you want to read later.

Pintereach helps you research by enabling you to save and organize articles in to categories to read later.

Create Boards

With Pintereach you can easily create various mood boards to hold articles that interest you. Simply create a board and name it as you wish.

Post Articles

Once you're logged in, simply click on post article and you can quickly post the article to one of your boards. You can upload your own cover image to make each article visually unique or use the article's own featured image.

Share Boards

Have a neat series that your friends may like? Grab the share link from your board to invite your friends to view your articles.
